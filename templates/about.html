About the Dashboard

The Madhya Pradesh Crime Dashboard is a data analytics and visualization platform designed to support evidence-based decision making for law enforcement, policymakers, and administrative authorities.

Purpose

This platform consolidates multi-year crime data published by government agencies and transforms it into interactive visual insights. The objective is to identify patterns, trends, and concentrations of crime across cities and states.

Data Sources

  • National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB)
  • Crime in India Reports (2019–2020)
  • Government Administrative Crime Data

Key Features

  • Year-wise and city-wise crime analysis
  • Age-group based offender distribution
  • Crime concentration indicators

Intended Users

The dashboard is intended for use by:

  • Government departments
  • Police leadership and analysts
  • Policy planners
  • Academic researchers

Development

This platform has been designed and developed using modern web technologies with a focus on performance, clarity, and scalability.

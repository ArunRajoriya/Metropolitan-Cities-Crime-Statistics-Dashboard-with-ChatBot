📚 Data Sources & Methodology

🏛️

National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB)

Official crime statistics from the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, covering comprehensive crime data across metropolitan cities.

📋

Crime in India Reports

Annual publications providing detailed crime statistics, trends, and analysis for the years 2016-2020 across various crime categories.

⚖️

Government Administrative Data

Official administrative records including case disposal statistics, investigation data, and judicial proceedings information.

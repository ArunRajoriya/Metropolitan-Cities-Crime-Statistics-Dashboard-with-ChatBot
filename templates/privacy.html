Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the Crime Analytics Dashboard.

1. No Personal Data Collection

This dashboard does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. It is a read-only analytical platform built using publicly available datasets.

2. Source of Data

All data displayed is sourced from publicly released NCRB reports and government publications. No private or sensitive information is used.

3. Cookies and Tracking

This application does not use cookies, tracking tools, analytics scripts, or third-party monitoring services.

4. External Links

If external links are provided, users are advised to review the privacy policies of those websites separately.

5. Data Accuracy

While efforts are made to represent the data accurately, users should verify information with official NCRB sources for authoritative usage.

6. Changes to Policy

This privacy policy may be updated without prior notice as the dashboard evolves.

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection on the Metropolitan Cities Crime Analytics Dashboard.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do NOT collect personal information. This dashboard operates as a read-only analytical platform and does not require user registration, login, or any form of personal data submission.

1.2 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous, non-identifiable usage statistics such as:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Browser type and version
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level only)

Note: This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

2. Data Sources

All crime statistics and data displayed on this platform are sourced exclusively from:

  • National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) official publications
  • Government of India public datasets
  • Publicly available statistical reports

No private, confidential, or personally identifiable information is used or displayed.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We do NOT use third-party advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, cross-site tracking technologies, or behavioral profiling tools.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including secure HTTPS encryption, regular security audits, and protection against common web vulnerabilities.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Feedback page.

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to transparency and responsible data practices.

# Crime Analytics Chatbot - Complete Capabilities Guide

## Overview
Our intelligent crime analytics chatbot can answer a wide variety of questions about crime data across Indian cities for years 2016, 2019, and 2020. Here's everything it can do:

## 🏙️ **City-Based Queries**

### Single City Analysis
- "Delhi arrests 2020" → Total arrests in Delhi for 2020
- "Mumbai crime statistics" → Complete crime data for Mumbai
- "Bangalore 2019" → All available data for Bangalore in 2019
- "Chennai arrests" → Latest year arrests for Chennai

### Multi-City Comparisons
- "Compare Delhi and Mumbai 2020" → Side-by-side comparison
- "Delhi vs Mumbai vs Kolkata" → Three-city comparison
- "Compare Indore with Pune" → Regional city comparison

## 👥 **Gender-Based Analysis**

## ❌ **What the Chatbot Cannot Do**

### Data Limitations
- No data before 2016 or after 2020
- No data for years 2017, 2018, 2021+
- Limited to Indian cities only
- No real-time or live data

### Query Limitations
- Cannot predict future crime trends
- Cannot provide legal advice or interpretations
- Cannot access external databases or APIs
- Cannot perform complex statistical modeling beyond basic analytics

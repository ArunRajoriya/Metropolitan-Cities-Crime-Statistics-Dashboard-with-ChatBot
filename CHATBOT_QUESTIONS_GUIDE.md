# Crime Analytics AI Chatbot - Complete Questions Guide

## Overview
The Crime Analytics AI Chatbot can answer questions about Indian crime data from the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) for years **2016, 2019, and 2020**. It covers multiple datasets including general crime data, government crime statistics, foreign crime data, and juvenile crime statistics.

## ⚠️ **LIMITATIONS & CONSTRAINTS**

### Data Limitations
- **Years Available**: Only 2016, 2019, 2020
- **Geographic Coverage**: Major Indian cities only
- **Data Source**: NCRB official records
- **Update Frequency**: Static historical data

### Query Limitations
- Cannot provide data for years outside 2016, 2019, 2020
- Cannot provide real-time or current data
- Cannot provide data for small towns/villages not in dataset
- Cannot provide international crime comparisons

### Response Capabilities
- Provides statistical analysis and insights
- Generates trends and comparisons
- Offers contextual suggestions
- Handles typos and abbreviations
- Remembers conversation context

## 📞 **GETTING HELP**

### If You're Stuck
- Ask "What can you help me with?"
- Try "Show me examples"
- Use "Help" or "Guide me"
- Ask "What data do you have?"

### Common Issues
- **City Not Found**: Check spelling or try major cities
- **Year Not Available**: Use 2016, 2019, or 2020
- **No Results**: Try broader queries or different cities
- **Unclear Response**: Rephrase your question more specifically
